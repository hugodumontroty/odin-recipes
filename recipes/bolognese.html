Fusilli Bolognese

Description

A tasty, warming dish of spaghetti bolognese, packed with veg to help you reach those 5 a day!

Ingredients

  • 300g beef mince
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely cut
  • 1 red onion, diced
  • 1 red pepper, deseeded and roughly diced
  • 1 stick of celery, thinly sliced
  • 100g fresh spinach
  • 200g mushrooms, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 500g tomato pasata
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 tsp Marmite
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 300g dried spaghetti
  • 50g grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Fry the onion, celery and garlic in half of the olive oil for 3-4 minutes, then add the mince and continue to fry until browned. Set aside.
  2. Fry the mushroom and peppers in the rest of the oil until soft, then add the mince mixture, pasata and stir in the tomato puree. Simmer gently for 5 minutes.
  3. Put the spaghetti on to boil in a saucepan. Add the carrot, spinach and Marmite to the bolognese sauce, stirring and leaving to simmer while the spaghetti cooks.
  4. Strain the spaghetti and serve with a healthy portion of the bolognese sauce and parmesan, if liked.
